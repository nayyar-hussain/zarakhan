About the Artist

A Journey of
Endless Discovery

Born in Lahore and trained at the National College of Arts, I've spent over a decade exploring the boundaries between realism and abstraction. My work is deeply rooted in the landscape, culture, and emotional texture of South Asia.

Every canvas begins with a feeling — a memory, a fragment of light, an emotion that refuses to stay silent. I paint not to represent the world, but to interpret it.

How I Work

The Creative
Process

1 Discovery

Concept & Vision

Every artwork begins with a deep conversation about your vision, the space, the emotion you want to evoke. We find the story together.

2 Sketching

Study & Sketch

Preliminary sketches and color studies bring the concept to life on paper before a single brushstroke touches the final canvas.

3 Creation

Painting & Refinement

Layer by layer, the painting breathes to life. I share progress photos throughout, inviting collaboration at every stage.

4 Delivery

Finishing & Framing

Varnished, framed, and hand-delivered. Each piece arrives ready to transform your space, with a certificate of authenticity.
